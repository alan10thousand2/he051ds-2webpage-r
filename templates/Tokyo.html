Japanese cuisine is diverse and delicious, from sushi and sashimi to ramen and tempura. Street food such as takoyaki and okonomiyaki also captivate and delight, making it hard to resist coming back for more.

Tokyo is located in the eastern part of the Kanto Plain, bordering Tokyo Bay. The area consists mainly of flat lowlands, ideal for large-scale urban development, while its network of rivers has historically played key roles in flood control and irrigation, shaping the city's unique landscape.
